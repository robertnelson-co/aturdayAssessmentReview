The Princess Bride

1987

This is a fun tribute page to my favorite movie!

The Princess Bride is the first movie I saw featuring Robin Wright. She is now one of my favorite actresses. Also, the character Inigo Montoya was my original inspiration to learn Spanish as my second language. There is even a joke in the script using a Spanish regional jargon!

  1. I learned the word "putrecense" from this movie.
  2. The video games featured in this movie were some of the first commercially available.
  3. There is no actual country called Giler, it was made up for the movie.
